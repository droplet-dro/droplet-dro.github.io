About Dro

「Dro」是「droplet」的縮寫，意思是一滴水。無論是知識、經驗，甚至人生的歷程，都是由一點一滴累積而成。這個名稱象徵着我們可以透過每一滴的積累，不斷成長，朝着「上善若水」的境界邁進。

Dro is short for “droplet”—a single drop of water. Just like knowledge, experience, and life itself, everything is built up drop by drop. This name reflects the belief that through these small accumulations, we grow continuously and strive to become someone who embodies the spirit of “supreme goodness is like water.”

目的 | Purpose

這個平台不僅僅是一個部落格，更是一個不斷成長的社群，建立在好奇心、洞察力與分享精神之上。在這裡，您將發現深思熟慮的觀點、實用的知識，以及可自由使用的資源，旨在激發靈感並支持您的探索旅程。

This platform is more than just a blog — it’s a growing community built around curiosity, insight, and generosity. Here, you’ll discover thoughtful reflections, practical knowledge, and freely accessible resources designed to spark ideas and support your journey.

意義 | Meaning

分享聽起來很簡單，但實際上受到許多複雜因素的影響：個人背景、知識深度、主觀偏見，以及理解的廣度與深度。真正的分享需要同理心、開放的態度，以及跨越差異的勇氣——這正是我希望在這裡培養的精神。

Sharing sounds simple, but in reality, it’s shaped by complex factors: personal backgrounds, levels of expertise, individual biases, and the scope or depth of understanding. True sharing requires empathy, openness, and the courage to bridge differences — and that’s exactly what we aim to cultivate here.

我分享的內容 | What We Share

我的內容涵蓋多個具變革性的領域，旨在激發好奇心、促進理解，並鼓勵有意義的對話。這個平台致力於分享即時洞察、深度觀點與實用資源，特別聚焦於商業、人工智慧與數據領域。

Our content spans a range of transformative domains, each chosen to spark curiosity, foster understanding, and encourage meaningful dialogue. This platform is dedicated to sharing timely insights, thoughtful perspectives, and practical resources — with a particular focus on Business, Artificial Intelligence, and Data.

自我介紹 | About me

我是一位資訊科技從業員，職業生涯始於軟件測試，隨後在IT項目執行與管理領域逐步發展。在這段期間，我由業務分析員開始累積經驗，之後轉型為項目經理。完成碩士學位後，我選擇踏上另一條跑道——前期銷售，展開了我在軟件測試、大數據分析和流程自動化方案前期銷售的大冒險。在這段旅程中，我學會了建立團隊、拓展人脈、追求自我成長，並協助公司拓展業務。

2019年，我決定專注於Data領域，發展個人興趣與專長。於是我從一家國際科技顧問公司轉職至一家以Data科技為核心的初創企業，擔任業務策略師。兩年後，我有幸加入國際大型科技公司Microsoft，成為技術銷售專員，專責推動Data與AI產品的業務發展。

這些經歷讓我深刻體會到科技發展日新月異，單向學習已難以追上其速度。正因如此，我更加重視「分享」的力量。透過分享，不僅能鞏固自身所學，也能讓他人有所收穫。

我希望透過分享各種事物，啟發更多人。我是一個以分享為樂的人，願意將每一滴累積的知識與經驗，化為成長的泉源。

I began my career in information technology as a software tester, gradually transitioning into IT project execution and management. During this phase, I gained valuable experience as a Business Analyst, which eventually led me to become a Project Manager. After completing my master’s degree, I boldly ventured into a new domain—presales—where I worked on solutions involving software testing, big data analytics, and process automation. This journey taught me how to build teams, expand networks, and pursue personal growth, all while helping the company scale its business.

In 2019, I decided to focus on the data domain, aligning my personal interests with professional growth. I moved from an international tech consulting firm to a data-centric startup, where I served as a Business Strategist. Two years later, I was fortunate to join Microsoft, a global technology leader, as a Technical Specialist responsible for driving business development in Data and AI solutions.

These experiences have made me deeply aware of how rapidly technology is evolving. Traditional one-way learning is no longer sufficient to keep pace. That’s when I discovered the power of sharing. By sharing what we learn, we reinforce our own understanding and help others grow as well.

I believe in the value of sharing and hope that through my contributions, others can benefit too. I find joy in sharing—and I’m here to grow together, one droplet at a time.
